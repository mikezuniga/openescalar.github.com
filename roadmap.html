Amun... Isis... Anubis...

There's a long path in front of us...


Which is the goal?

Provide all cloud services out there in an open source manner that will allow everyone to make use of them either through public/private or hybrid clouds... In short words provide compatible cloud services on any cloud.


To where from here?

First step is to move through Amun Clouds Management to control our IaaS and create infrastructures which can be deployed through multiple clouds. Later on when we release Isis (our PaaS management), users will be able to make use of infrastructures created by Amun. Finally Anubis (our SaaS integrator) will allow you to interconnect through basic services multiple PaaS managed by Anubis under a same control making your applications completely transparent to the users.


So the big picture is?

Use Amun to control and manage instances through multiple clouds.
Allow developers to use Platform as a Service managed by Isis built on top of Amun.
Give your users access to your applications and integrate them using Anubis.
